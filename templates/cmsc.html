About Us

Chakdara Model School & College (CMSC), established in July 2002, is a prestigious English medium institution dedicated to delivering quality education.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our students' outstanding academic achievements, extracurricular successes, and participation in inter-school competitions. With a purpose-built campus and a legacy of excellence, CMSC continues to set new benchmarks in education.

Our Aim: We Give you the Opportunity today!

Our mission is to nurture responsible, patriotic Pakistanis and true Muslims by instilling strong academic values and character.

At CMSC, we aim to provide an education that shapes not just scholars but responsible citizens.We are committed to upholding high academic standards, encouraging extracurricular excellence, and offering state-of-the-art learning facilities.
